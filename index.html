Neil Josten

Neil Josten is the main character of the All for the Game series. He has been on the run from his father the a serial killer with the moniker of the Butcher of Baltimore. When he is offered a position on the Palmetto State University Exy team he has to choose between his love for exy and staying hidden from his father.

On a team known for giving its players a second chance Neil is not the only one with secrets, and when one of his teamates is a friend from his past Neil finds out there is more to the story. With lies stacked like a house of cards he decides that after 8 years on the run it might be time to fight for something or someone for a change. Will his lies crumble around him? and will he survive?
